Team-Based Learning (TBL) Reflection

What is Team-Based Learning?

We worked as a permanent team of 4 members. Everyone first studied alone, then we met, discussed problems, built the website together and gave each other feedback. It was much better than working alone!

What We Learned as a Team

  • Planning on paper first saves a lot of time
  • Always close your tags properly!
  • Using the same heading sizes makes the site look neat
  • Every image needs alt text
  • We must test on phones too
  • Short daily check-ins prevent big problems
