Pairwise Dance Judging: How Data Fuels Fair AI Rankings


This web is developed for users to explore our project.

It allows users to understand how we use AI to rank dance performances fairly through pairwise comparisons by playing and judging the simple videos.

The result will show the differences between AI ranking and human ranking based on their judgements, with the final reflection on whether AI is trustworthy in those rating.
